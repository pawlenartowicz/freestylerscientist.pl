My name is Paweł Lenartowicz and I'm taking my first steps in science. Devoted to statistics and metascience, I lean towards social sciences and psychology. I'm an independent scientist, the founder of the Society for Open Science. I have developed the LRBT method for detecting publication bias as well as the MCPower package for Monte Carlo power analysis for linear regression. I also conduct workshops in statistics. I love cooking, avant-garde jazz and sailing, and live la vida loca between Warsaw and Krakow.
