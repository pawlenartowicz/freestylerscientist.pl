Hi! I'm an independent scientist, working my best to make statistics more accessible and lovable to everyone. I believe that good statistical tools and education shouldn't be limited by unnecessary complexity, mathematical jargon, or programming requirements. I spend my time creating tools and educational resources that could help scientists and students better understand and simply use statistics.

Thanks to your support, I can:
  • host servers with statistical tools – so they can be available for everyone without coding;
  • build Society for Open Science – so advancing research that benefits as many people – both scientists, students and the community – can become possible;
  • create educational content – so my passion and goal of providing free workshops and materials and enabling people from different scientific backgrounds to better understand statistics could live forward;
  • and of course spend more time developing existing and creating new methods! :)
